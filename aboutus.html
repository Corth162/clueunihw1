About Us

Here at Cluedo University we are dedicated to the study and research of Computer Science and its related fields. Our faculty strives to deepen students' expertise in designing, developing, and analyzing both software and hardware. Our mission is to equip graduates with the skills and knowledge to excel in any industry—whether in business, engineering, academia, research, or freelance innovation.
