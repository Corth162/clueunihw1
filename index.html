Welcome to Cluedo
University

Welcome to Cluedo University, a distinguished institution where innovation meets tradition. Founded in 1949 by our first ever Headmaster Anthony Pratt, whose groundbreaking concept for Cluedo was first conceived in 1943. Our university has grown from humble beginnings into a center of academic excellence. We are a unique school, in that we specialize in Computer Science and its branches of related study. Join us and be part of a legacy dedicated to transformative research, creative problem-solving, and a commitment to shaping the future of technology.
